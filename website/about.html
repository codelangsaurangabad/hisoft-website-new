About Hisoft IT Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

A Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar based IT company delivering business software, websites, Android apps, ERP systems, billing tools, payment integrations and digital growth services.

Who we are

Hisoft IT Solutions Pvt. Ltd. works with schools, colleges, clinics, hospitals, offices, petrol pumps, retailers and service businesses that need reliable digital systems for everyday operations. The company focuses on practical software that helps teams manage admissions, billing, stock, accounts, documents, payments, marketing enquiries and reporting from one organized workflow.

Our work covers both product-based solutions and custom development. We build school management software, college management software, college dynamic websites, GST billing software, ERP modules, office management software, file management systems, hospital and clinic management applications, Android apps and payment gateway connected platforms.

Every project starts with understanding the client’s real process: forms, registers, reports, approvals, staff roles, customer communication and the way daily work is currently handled. Based on that, Hisoft plans a clean system structure, builds the required screens and backend, prepares public pages where needed, and supports deployment with training and technical assistance.

How we work

We prefer clear modules, simple data entry, secure admin access and reports that owners can actually use. A typical project may include a responsive website, an admin dashboard, Android app access, email or SMS notifications, payment gateway flow, Play Store policy pages and SEO-ready public content.

For growing organizations, Hisoft can extend systems over time. A website can later connect to admissions. Billing can later connect to inventory and accounts. A custom dashboard can later include branch-wise reports, user permissions, reminders and exportable data.

Support is part of the relationship. We help clients with updates, small improvements, policy URLs for app submission, hosting guidance, content changes and day-to-day troubleshooting so the technology keeps serving the business after launch.

Mission

To deliver affordable, reliable and easy-to-use technology for Indian businesses that need daily operational clarity, better customer communication and structured digital records.

Vision

To become a trusted regional software partner for cloud-ready business systems, mobile workflows, education technology, healthcare software, compliance-first apps and SEO-friendly websites.

Leadership and team

Leadership, development, quality assurance and digital support teams work together on client IT projects from planning to launch and maintenance.
