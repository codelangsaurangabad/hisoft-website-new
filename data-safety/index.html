Data Safety Declaration

A public summary for apps published under Hisoft IT Solutions Pvt. Ltd. Actual app declarations should be reviewed per app before submission.

Data collected

Depending on the app, data may include name, phone, email, user IDs, business records, tour details, expenses, photos uploaded by users, device diagnostics and app interactions.

Purpose

Data supports login, account management, business reports, approvals, customer support, fraud prevention, app functionality and analytics.

Sharing

Data may be processed by cloud hosting, analytics, payment, messaging and support providers. We do not sell user data.

Security practices

Apps should use secure transport, access-controlled dashboards, credential protection and limited admin access based on project requirements.

Deletion requests

Users can request account or data deletion at info@hisofttechnology.com. Requests should include app name, registered mobile number and account identifier.

App-specific notes

Tour, claim and business apps may store operational records required by employers or account administrators. Retention may depend on business/legal obligations.
